Example Persona

Name: Sam
Age: 23
Job: Casual labourer
English proficency: OK

Bio

Sam spent his early years in foster care homes and now moves around to find employment. He has learning difficulties, was diagnosed with ADHD when he was young and left school without any formal qualifications.

Sam manages to get by doing short, cash in hand jobs, including gardening, though it’s hard to find regular work.

Sam ‘signs on’ every fortnight but finds it very difficult to look for jobs online. He completed the short internet skills course at the Jobcentre but found it overwhelming. Combined with his low self-esteem Sam feels less confident about using the computers in the local library.

He can't afford to call expensive phone numbers from his mobile phone and his lack of fixed abode and access to the Internet currently limits his chance to improve his circumstances.

Internet use

Has not had access to the internet personally before, but uses the internt to search for jobs via JobCenter worker.

Barriers
Digital literacy Low skills
Literacy Poor reading
Content literacy Familiar with benefits system
Finance Can’t afford new devices. Has a PAYG feature phone
Accessibility Learning difficulties
