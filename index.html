Map the research participants of [Firearms licensing]

What

This tool is to assist you in plotting and recording your research participants against the Digital Inclusion Scale. It is a short series of questions that should take no longer than three minutes per participant.

How

This tool is to be used after your session with the research participant.

During the research session you will need to find out the following in order to ensure you can complete the questions successfully:

  • If they have ever, and continue to use the internet?
  • Their attitudes towards being online, whether they like it, or use it reluctantly
  • Whether they need help with online tasks such as using a search engine, buying things or using emails
  • If they have advanced digital skills?

Why

The outcome of each plotted participant will be recorded and displayed in a dashboard that you can view, or create a downloadable copy at any time. It will show the spread of the research participants you are achieving.

The scale is used for service assessments, to understand how inclusive your research is when recruiting, and to give an overview of how research of a particular project is progressing.

More information about the Digital Inclusion scale can be found at https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/government- digital-inclusion-strategy/government-digital-inclusion-strategy
